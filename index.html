About Alice

Highly perceptive, adaptable, and dedicated to every child's progress

Hi, I'm Alice. My approach is thoughtful and tailored to the individual — I am highly perceptive to each child's needs, consistently adapting my methods to suit their learning style, their pace, and their personality.

Whether a child needs to break things down into smaller steps, take a movement break to stay focused, or simply needs more time to build trust before they'll try something new — I notice, and I respond. I have particular experience supporting children who have fallen behind academically, as well as those with additional needs including ADHD, helping them regain confidence, rebuild self-esteem, and make excellent academic progress.

My professionalism and patience mean children feel genuinely safe in our sessions, and parents often remark on how quickly even the most anxious children settle in and start to thrive. The moment a child shows their parent their work with pride — that is what drives everything I do.

What I Offer

Tutoring that adapts to the whole child

🎯

A Thoughtful, Tailored Approach

Every session is built entirely around your child. I adapt my methods to suit their learning style, their pace, and their personality — no two children are taught the same way, because no two children are the same.

📚

Literacy & Core Skills

From reading and writing to comprehension and expression, I build strong foundations that give children confidence across all their subjects — skills that stay with them long after our sessions end.

💛

Confidence & Self-Esteem

Many children I work with arrive having fallen behind or struggling with low confidence. I create a safe, encouraging environment where they can rebuild belief in themselves and make excellent academic progress.

🧠

ADHD & Additional Needs

I have significant experience supporting children with ADHD and other additional needs, adapting sessions to maintain focus — including knowing when a child benefits from a movement or exercise break rather than pushing on.

🌱

Progress You Can See

Children love to show their parents the work they've done each week — and parents consistently remark on the difference they see, both academically and in their child's confidence and self-belief.

🤝

Support for Parents

I believe parents are essential partners in a child's education. I offer honest, practical guidance throughout, so you always understand how your child is progressing and how best to support them at home.
