About Alice

25 years of experience, a passion for every child

Hi, I'm Alice. I am a fully qualified KS2 primary school teacher with 25 years of experience working with children across London and Kent — in state schools, prep schools, grammar schools, and in private tutoring. Having taught in both the state and prep systems, I have a deep understanding of the teaching strategies and styles used across all of them.

I currently tutor children with SEND needs, prepare students for the London 11+ and Kent Test, and work with children who need to catch up or simply want a boost in confidence. I also offer impartial advice on schools across Kent and London — state, grammar, and private — having seen them from the inside.

My own children (now 17 and 18) have sat the 11+ grammar exams and private school entrance exams, so I understand both sides — the pressures on children, the challenge of keeping them motivated, and the importance of protecting their well-being, activities, and family life throughout.

What I Offer

What I can help with

11+ & Kent Test Preparation

I prepare students for both the London school 11+ and the Kent Test. I have a broad knowledge of resources — from Atom Learning and Bond & CGP books to private school online tests — and tailor the approach to what works best for each child.

SEND & Additional Needs

I have significant experience tutoring children with SEND needs, including ADHD. I adapt every session to maintain focus, and know when a child benefits from a movement break rather than pushing through.

Catching Up & Building Confidence

Many children I work with arrive having fallen behind or struggling with low self-esteem. I create a safe, encouraging space where they can rebuild belief in themselves and make excellent academic progress.

Learning Strategies for Life

I teach children about their different learning styles and help them discover how they learn best. These are strategies they take forward into secondary school — not just tools for passing a test.

Impartial School Advice

Having worked inside state, grammar, and private schools in both Kent and London, I can offer honest, impartial advice on different schools, informal and formal assessments, and what to expect from each sector.

Supporting Parents at Home

I am happy for parents to sit in on sessions — and I actively encourage it. I can teach you the same strategies so you can support your child's learning at home. We work best together.

My Approach

No lesson I teach is ever the same

I constantly adapt to the pace of the child and have resources on hand to change direction the moment something needs more clarification — or we find a better way in. I listen to what each child tells me works for them, and then I build from there.

01

Understanding how your child learns

I teach children about their different learning styles and we work out together what suits them best — whether that's Atom Learning, Bond & CGP books, private school online tests, or a completely different approach. No two children are alike, and no two sessions are alike.

02

Strategies they carry with them forever

I don't just help children pass a test — I teach learning strategies they can take forward into secondary school and beyond. The skills we build in our sessions are skills for life, not just for the exam.

03

Protecting the whole child

I strongly advocate doing homework in the morning before school — children are too tired after a full day, and clubs, playdates, and family time should never be sidelined. I have lived through this with my own children, and I understand the importance of balance and well-being above all else.

04

Parents are always welcome

I am happy for parents to sit in on sessions and I actively encourage it. I can walk you through the same strategies so you are equipped to support your child at home. The more we work together as a team, the better the results.
